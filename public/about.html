About Me

The author, Mr. Rodante R. Reyes, was born on August 29, 1975, in Barangay Inchican, Silang, Cavite. He is married to Mrs. Mylène O. Reyes, and they have a daughter, Geinevive Reyes. They are currently residing in Trece Martires City, Cavite. In 1989, he completed his elementary education at Don Jose Elementary School in Sta. Rosa, Laguna, and his secondary education at Don Jose National High School in 1993. In 1995, he earned a vocational certificate in Electronics Technology from Saint Louis Anne Academy in San Pedro, Laguna. After working for several years, he resumed his education and obtained a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering from AMA Computer University in Makati City in 2003. From 2003 to 2018, Mr. Reyes worked in various companies and later became the IT Manager at Innotor Philippines, Inc. in Calamba, Laguna. In 2021, he separated from the company due to manpower retrenchment during the pandemic. In 2018, he pursued a master’s degree in Information Technology at Cavite State University – Main Campus, focusing his research on employee performance evaluation system and daily time record. In 2021, he joined Seiwa Kaiun Philippines, Inc. in General Trias, Cavite as an IT Manager, where he is currently employed. He obtained his master’s degree in August 2025. .
